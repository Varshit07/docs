List your clusters
==================

To get the list of your clusters, use the ``hasura`` CLI.

.. code-block:: bash

  $ hasura clusters list

  INFO Getting clusters list...
  Clusters available In your account:
  NO   NAME         OWNER
  1    alarming52   you

  Clusters added to this project:
  NO   NAME         ALIAS
  1    alarming52   hasura
